Covid-19 India Dashboard:



This is a Covid-19 India Dashboard providing an overview of the 2020 Novel Covid-19 virus outbreak in India and elsewhere around the world.This dashboard is build using Python on django framework.

Source of Data:


The multiple sources of data is pulled from amazing guys at John Hopkins University CSSE, Indian Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, as well as other reliable news sources and goverment press releases. The data and dashboard are refreshed on a daily basis.
